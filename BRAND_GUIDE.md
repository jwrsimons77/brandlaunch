## üñºÔ∏è Image & Visual Direction

### Photography Style Recommendations

**Option 1: High-Energy Action Shots**
- Dynamic movement captures
- Sweat, intensity, determination
- Urban/outdoor settings
- Example: CrossFit, HIIT, boxing-style content

**Option 2: Lifestyle & Community**
- Group shots, smiling faces
- Post-workout hangs, coffee meet-ups
- Candid, authentic moments
- Example: ClassPass, SoulCycle-style content

**Option 3: Cinematic & Editorial**
- Moody lighting, dramatic angles
- Fashion-forward athletic wear
- Aspirational imagery
- Example: Lululemon, Nike-style content

**Option 4: Minimal & Clean**
- Simple backgrounds
- Focus on form and technique
- Muted tones, lots of white space
- Example: Apple Fitness, Peloton-style content

### Hero Section Options

1. **Video Background** - High-energy workout footage (loop 10-15 seconds)
2. **Static Image** - Bold, full-screen fitness lifestyle shot
3. **Slideshow** - Rotate through 3-4 community images
4. **Gradient Overlay** - Solid color with texture

### Image Sources

**Free Stock Photos:**
- [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com/s/photos/fitness) - High-quality, free images
- [Pexels](https://pexels.com/search/workout/) - Great fitness content
- [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com/images/search/gym/) - Large selection

**Paid Stock Photos:**
- [Adobe Stock](https://stock.adobe.com) - Professional quality
- [Shutterstock](https://shutterstock.com) - Huge library
- [Getty Images](https://gettyimages.com) - Premium content

**Tips:**
- Search terms: "fitness lifestyle", "workout community", "athletic training"
- Look for diversity in body types and ethnicities
- Prefer natural lighting and authentic moments
- Avoid overly staged or cheesy stock photos

## üí° Pro Tips

### Brand Consistency
- Use the same color scheme across all platforms
- Maintain consistent voice and tone
- Create brand guidelines document
- Use templates for social media posts

### Community Building
- Respond to every DM and comment
- Feature community members on your platform
- Create a unique hashtag (#YourBrandName)
- Host regular events to maintain momentum

### Content Strategy
- Post 3-5x per week on Instagram
- Mix content types (workout tips, community spotlights, event promos)
- Use Instagram Stories daily
- Create Reels/TikToks for algorithm boost

### Growth Hacks
- Partner with local fitness influencers
- Offer founding member benefits
- Run referral programs
- Collaborate with complementary brands (healthy cafes, athletic wear)
