## üñºÔ∏è Required Images

### Hero Section (`/images/hero/`)

**hero-bg.jpg**
- **Dimensions:** 1920x1080px (Full HD)
- **Description:** Main hero background image
- **Style:** Bold, energetic fitness action shot or lifestyle image
- **Alternative:** Can be replaced with video (hero-video.mp4)
- **Format:** JPG or WebP
- **Max Size:** 500KB (optimized)

**og-image.jpg**
- **Dimensions:** 1200x630px
- **Description:** Social media sharing image (Open Graph)
- **Style:** Your brand logo/name with engaging background
- **Format:** JPG
- **Max Size:** 300KB

### About Section (`/images/about/`)

**about-image.jpg**
- **Dimensions:** 1200x1200px (Square)
- **Description:** Community or training image for about section
- **Style:** Authentic, engaging, shows community or action
- **Format:** JPG or WebP
- **Max Size:** 400KB

### Events Section (`/images/events/`)

You need **4 event images**:

**event-1.jpg** (Featured Event)
- **Dimensions:** 800x600px (4:3 ratio)
- **Description:** Primary/featured event image
- **Example:** Beach HIIT workout, sunrise training

**event-2.jpg**
- **Dimensions:** 800x600px
- **Description:** Second event
- **Example:** Rooftop yoga session

**event-3.jpg**
- **Dimensions:** 800x600px
- **Description:** Third event
- **Example:** Strength training challenge

**event-4.jpg** (Coming Soon)
- **Dimensions:** 800x600px
- **Description:** Upcoming event
- **Example:** Weekend retreat or special event

### Gallery Section (`/images/gallery/`)

You need **9 gallery images** for the Instagram-style grid:

**gallery-1.jpg through gallery-9.jpg**
- **Dimensions:** 600x600px (Square format)
- **Description:** Community moments, training shots, lifestyle images
- **Style:** Mix of action shots, group photos, and lifestyle content
- **Format:** JPG or WebP
- **Max Size:** 200KB each

**Content Mix Suggestions:**
- 3 action/workout shots
- 3 community/group photos
- 2 lifestyle moments (post-workout, social)
- 1 inspirational/aspirational shot

### Icons (`/images/icons/`)

**favicon.png**
- **Dimensions:** 512x512px (will be resized automatically)
- **Description:** Website favicon/icon
- **Format:** PNG with transparency
- **Style:** Your brand logo or simplified icon

**Optional:**
- favicon-16x16.png
- favicon-32x32.png
- apple-touch-icon.png (180x180px)

## üé® Image Style Guidelines

### Overall Aesthetic
- **Mood:** Energetic, bold, aspirational
- **Colors:** Should complement your brand colors
- **Lighting:** Natural light preferred, high contrast okay
- **People:** Diverse, authentic, engaged
- **Locations:** Urban, outdoor, unique spaces

### Photography Tips
1. **Authenticity** - Avoid overly staged stock photos
2. **Diversity** - Represent various body types, ages, ethnicities
3. **Energy** - Capture movement and emotion
4. **Context** - Show your unique training environments
5. **Quality** - High resolution, well-lit, properly exposed

### What to Avoid
- ‚ùå Cheesy stock photos with fake smiles
- ‚ùå Overly edited/filtered images
- ‚ùå Low resolution or pixelated images
- ‚ùå Inconsistent style across sections
- ‚ùå Images with visible watermarks

## üîß Image Optimization

Before uploading, optimize your images:

### Online Tools (Free)
- [TinyPNG](https://tinypng.com) - Compress PNG and JPG
- [Squoosh](https://squoosh.app) - Advanced compression
- [ImageOptim](https://imageoptim.com) - Mac app
- [Optimizilla](https://imageoptim.com/online) - Batch compression

### Optimization Checklist
- [ ] Resize to required dimensions
- [ ] Compress to reduce file size
- [ ] Convert to WebP for better performance (optional)
- [ ] Test loading speed after upload
- [ ] Ensure images are sharp (not blurry)

### Target File Sizes
- Hero: 300-500KB
- Events: 150-300KB
- Gallery: 100-200KB each
- Icons: <50KB

## üì∑ Where to Source Images

### Option 1: Original Photography
**Recommended for authenticity**
- Hire a local photographer for a shoot
- Use high-quality smartphone camera
- Organize a photo day with your community
- **Cost:** $200-500 for photographer, free for DIY

### Option 2: Free Stock Photos
**Good for getting started quickly**

**Unsplash** (unsplash.com)
- Search: "fitness", "workout", "gym", "athletic"
- High quality, free to use
- No attribution required (but appreciated)

**Pexels** (pexels.com)
- Search: "fitness lifestyle", "training", "community workout"
- Large selection
- Free with no attribution required

**Pixabay** (pixabay.com)
- Search: "fitness", "sport", "exercise"
- Good variety
- Free license

### Option 3: Paid Stock Photos
**For premium, specific content**

**Adobe Stock** (stock.adobe.com)
- Professional quality
- $29.99 for 10 images/month

**Shutterstock** (shutterstock.com)
- Huge library
- Plans from $29/month

**iStock** (istockphoto.com)
- Good selection
- Credit-based pricing

### Search Terms to Use
- "fitness lifestyle"
- "workout community"
- "athletic training"
- "gym workout"
- "outdoor fitness"
- "yoga class"
- "HIIT training"
- "strength training"
- "fitness group"
- "active lifestyle"

## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Consistency is Key** - Use similar lighting and color grading across all images
2. **Update Regularly** - Refresh gallery images monthly to show active community
3. **User-Generated Content** - Feature real members once you have them
4. **Seasonal Updates** - Change hero image seasonally to keep site fresh
5. **Test Mobile** - Ensure images look good on small screens
