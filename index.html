Who We Are

Running gear. Quality coffee.

AORO is a UK running brand built on community runs and coffee pop-ups. From the Peak District to London and Wales, we bring runners together for group runs, quality tees, and coffee after every effort.

Two friends started this between Saturday morning runs and coffee. Now we're building something bigger—limited tee drops with meaning, pop-ups across the Peak District, London, and Wales, and a crew that turns up rain or shine.

Coffee gets served from the car. Runs start wherever the crew meets. Every tee is made to last. If you run with others, you're already part of this.

What We Stand For

Run Together

Two things we believe: quality over quantity, and running's better with mates.

Community over competition

Running isn't about being first. It's about showing up, putting in the work, and having people there when it gets hard. We build gear for the crew, not the podium.

Coffee after runs

The run ends when the coffee's done. We serve it from the car, keep it simple, and never rush the debrief. Quality coffee, quality conversations.
