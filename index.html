Who We Are

Adventure-ready tees. Quality coffee. Unforgettable mates.

AORO is for those who'd rather be outside. Named after Aoraki—New Zealand's highest peak, meaning "Cloud Piercer"—we're building more than a brand. We're building a basecamp.

Two mates started this between trail markers and coffee stops. Now it's your turn to join the climb. Expect limited gear that actually keeps up, coffee worth brewing twice, and a crew that shows up rain or shine.

Every drop, every pop-up, every adventure is crafted to pierce the ordinary. If you're chasing higher ground, you're already one of us.
